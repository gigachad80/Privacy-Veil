Getting Started

  1. Click the extension icon in your toolbar to open the control panel
  2. Toggle dark mode on/off and adjust brightness to your preference
  3. Visit any website - dark mode is automatically applied
  4. If a site looks broken, click "Exclude Current Site" in the popup
  5. Right-click on any page for quick access to exclusion options
